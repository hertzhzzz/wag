## 通话前准备

**心态**：这不是销售电话——是信息收集。你在了解他们的采购现状，不是卖东西。

**记录工具**：准备好笔记本或手机，记录每个客户的：
- 当前供应商是谁
- 每年采购金额大概多少
- 最大痛点
- 对免费报价的兴趣度（1-10分）
- 对去中国看厂的意愿度（1-10分）

---

## 开场白（30秒，照着念）

> "Hi, this is [你的名字] from Winning Adventure Global. I help Australian SME owners access better pricing directly from verified Chinese factories — and I arrange and coordinate the factory visit in China myself.
>
> I'm not selling anything today — I'm just looking to understand how businesses like yours currently source their equipment. Do you have 5 minutes for a few quick questions?"

**如果对方说"没时间"**：
> "I completely understand — just one quick question: who do you currently buy your [equipment type] from, and are you happy with the pricing?"

**如果对方同意继续** → 进入问题

---

## Question 1 — 当前采购情况

**目的**：了解他们买什么、从谁那里买、付多少钱

> "First, what equipment do you currently source, and who do you buy from? Just generally — I'm trying to understand the market."

**记录**：
- 产品类别（auto parts / AV equipment / lighting / sound 等）
- 供应商名称
- 大概采购金额范围

---

## Question 2 — 最大痛点

**目的**：找到他们不爽的地方

> "What's your biggest frustration with your current suppliers — if you have any?"

**可能的回答**：
- 价格太贵
- 质量问题
- 交货慢
- 沟通困难
- 配件不好找

**记录痛点**

---

## Question 3 — 核心试探（最重要）

**目的**：测试两件事：①对免费报价的兴趣 ②对去中国看厂的意愿

> "If I could get you a better price quote directly from a factory — free, no commitment, no obligation — and show you exactly how much you could save compared to your current supplier... would that be interesting to hear more about?"

**等对方回应**

如果对方说"Yes, interesting"：
> "And if the numbers made sense — if the factory quote was meaningfully lower than what you're paying now — would you consider flying to China to see the factory in person? I personally coordinate the whole trip: flights, hotel, translator, factory appointments — everything arranged."

**记录**：
- 对免费报价的兴趣度（1-10）
- 对去中国看厂的意愿度（1-10）

---

## 结束语

**如果对方有兴趣**：
> "Great. Can I follow up with you next week? I'll have a specific quote to share — just need to know what products you're looking for. What's the best way to reach you?"

**如果对方没兴趣**：
> "No problem at all — thank you for your time. If anything changes or you think about sourcing differently, I'd be happy to help. Have a great day."
