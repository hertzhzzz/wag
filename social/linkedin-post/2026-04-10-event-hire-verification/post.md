# LinkedIn Post: Event Hire Industry China Verification

## Hook (198 chars)
Before renting event equipment from a new supplier, verify three things: factory, not trading company.

## Body

Step 1 — Check the business license on gsxt.gov.cn
Does the business scope list "manufacturing" (生产) or just "wholesale" (贸易)? Trading companies cannot manufacture.

Step 2 — Request live video of active production lines
Professional photos can be staged. Ask for live footage during China working hours (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm).

Step 3 — Verify certifications directly with issuing bodies
CE and ISO certificates presented as PDF files often fail direct verification. Check directly with the certification body.
