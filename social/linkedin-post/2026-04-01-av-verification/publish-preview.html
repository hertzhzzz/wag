Before you buy your next batch of pro audio gear from China, verify three things: factory, not trading company. Step 1 -- Check the business license on gsxt.gov.cn Look for "manufacturing" or "production" in the business scope. Trading companies show "wholesale" or "trade" -- not a factory. Step 2 -- Request a live video of production during working hours Polished websites and sample rooms can be rented. Live footage of active production lines cannot be faked. Step 3 -- Verify certifications directly with the issuing body Accepting certificate files is how trading companies win trust. Call or email the certification body to confirm validity.
