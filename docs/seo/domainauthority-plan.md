## What is Domain Authority?

Domain Authority (DA) is a Moz metric (0-100) that predicts how well a website will rank on search engines. It's based on:
- Number of linking root domains
- Quality of linking domains
- Age of domain
- Size of link profile

**Current WAG DA:** ~5 (Moz)
**Target DA:** 15+

---

## How to Check DA

### Method 1: Moz Free Bar (Quickest)

1. Install MozBar browser extension (free)
2. Visit winningadventure.com.au
3. See DA displayed in browser toolbar

### Method 2: Ahrefs Webmaster Tools (Recommended)

1. Go to https://ahrefs.com/webmaster-tools
2. Add your site and verify ownership
3. View Domain Rating (DR) - similar to DA

**Bookmark:** Check DA monthly during audit workflow.
