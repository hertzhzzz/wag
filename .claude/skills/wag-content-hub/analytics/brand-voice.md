## Voice Guidelines

### Speaker Position
- **WAG = The knowledgeable guide** — Not the seller, not the buyer
- Speak from experience: "In our work with Australian SMEs..."
- Position: Peer who has seen this many times, offering hard-won lessons

### Language Rules
- **No emoji** — None, anywhere, ever
- **No "WA"** — Use "Winning Adventure Global" or "WAG"
- **No Chinese** in published posts — English only for posts
  - Exception: Blog MDX may contain Chinese (for Chinese-speaking audiences)
- **No jargon** — Explain concepts for educated non-experts
- **Numbers over opinions** — "85% of Australian SMBs..." beats "most businesses..."

### Content Priorities (by ER performance)

| Priority | Format | ER Benchmark |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | How-to / Step-by-step frameworks | ER up to 33% |
| 2 | Case studies with specific examples | ER 10-20% |
| 3 | Tactical advice with named tools | ER 8-15% |
| 4 | Myth-busting (only with actionable framework) | ER 5-10% |
| 5 | Brand intro | ER <5% |

### CTA Rules (Data-Proven)

| CTA Type | Performance | Use Instead Of |
|----------|------------|---------------|
| Experience-based questions | High comments | "What would you do?" (0 comments) |
| Specific yes/no with nuance | High comments | "Share in comments" (passive) |
| War story requests | High engagement | "Let's discuss" (vague) |
| Complete the sentence | High replies | "Any questions?" (dead end) |

**DO NOT use:**
- "What would you do differently?" — 0 comments historically
- "Share in comments" — passive, no trigger
- "Let's discuss" — vague, no specific angle
- "Any questions?" — conversation dead end

### Hashtag Rules

| Platform | Limit | Strategy |

| X | 1-2 | One broad + one niche |

## Platform Voice Adjustments

### LinkedIn
- Tone: Professional but approachable
- Voice: Educational, peer-to-peer
- Pronouns: "We" for company, "I" for personal founder voice
- Length: 150-300 words + hook (210 char max)

### X/Twitter
- Tone: Sharp, direct, expert
- Voice: Provocative but factual
- Pronouns: "We" or brand name
- Length: 280 chars max, standalone hooks

### Facebook
- Tone: Community, relationship-driven
- Voice: "We've all been there..." / peer stories
- Pronouns: "We" or "I"
- Length: Flexible, story-driven

### SEO/Blog
- Tone: Educational, comprehensive
- Voice: Authoritative guide
- Pronouns: "You" (reader) + "We" (WAG)
- Length: 1500-3000 words

## Content Type Performance

**Worst-performing hook formula:**
