## Hook Types by Channel

| Hook Type | Description | LinkedIn | X/Twitter | Facebook | SEO Blog |
|-----------|-------------|----------|-----------|----------|----------|
| **knowledge-type** | Share actionable expertise | ★★★ | ★★ | ★★ | ★★★ |
| **sharpness-test** | Provocative claim or stat | ★★ | ★★★ | ★ | ★ |
| **relationship-type** | Personal/emotional connection | ★★ | ★ | ★★★ | ★ |
| **long-tail** | Comprehensive, evergreen | ★ | ★ | ★ | ★★★ |
| **question-type** | Engage with open question | ★★ | ★★★ | ★★★ | ★ |
| **data-type** | Charts, numbers, research | ★★★ | ★★★ | ★★ | ★★★ |

### Hook Usage Guidelines

**knowledge-type** (primary for WAG):
- Frame as "here's what I've learned" or "what most [companies/people] miss"
- Include specific steps, checklists, or frameworks
- End with invitation: "drop a comment if..."
- Best for: factory verification, quality control, sourcing tips

**sharpness-test** (for X/LinkedIn reach):
- Lead with controversial or counterintuitive claim
- "X% of Australia-based companies don't know this about..."
- Requires data backing — cite sources
- Best for: driving shares, reaching new audiences

**relationship-type** (for Facebook/community):
- First-person narrative: "My first factory visit in Shenzhen..."
- Behind-the-scenes, human stories
- Best for: brand building, community trust

## Cross-Channel Content Adaptation Rules

### LinkedIn → X/Twitter
- Strip to core insight (280 chars)
- Remove personal pronouns ("I've seen" → "Companies often")
- Add data point or provocative stat if available
- Change CTA from "comment" to "quote tweet"

### LinkedIn → Facebook
- Add personal anecdote opener
- Shift to community-focused framing ("our community has found...")
- Increase emotional resonance
- Change CTA to engagement question

### Blog → LinkedIn
- Extract 3 key points as carousel slides
- Lead with hook stat or provocative question
- Add "save this post" or "comment your approach"
- Include blog link at end (not as primary)

### Blog → X/Twitter
- Extract single most surprising finding
- Lead with the number or stat
- Include blog link in thread, not main tweet
- Thread the supporting points
