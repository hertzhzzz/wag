Perth businesses serving these sectors often face a structural disadvantage: they are 2,000 kilometres from the nearest major supplier precincts, and Eastern States distributors add substantial margins to imported goods.

Direct factory sourcing from China addresses that disadvantage directly. By working with WAG to establish manufacturer relationships, Perth businesses can access factory pricing while maintaining professional quality verification.

## Why Perth Businesses Are Direct-Sourcing from China

Perth businesses that have historically relied on Australian distributors of imported equipment are finding that those distributors themselves source from China, add margin, and pass the cost on. The logic of going direct is straightforward: cut out the intermediary that exists between you and the actual manufacturer.

The practical barrier has always been verification and logistics. Perth business owners cannot afford to spend two weeks in China on a speculative visit, manage factory communication in Mandarin, and arrange international freight independently. WAG handles all of that infrastructure.
