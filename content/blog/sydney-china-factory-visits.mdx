## Why Sydney Businesses Need Direct Factory Access

The Australian import market has matured. Businesses that sourced through Alibaba and trading companies in the early 2010s are now facing margin pressure as competition intensifies. The businesses pulling ahead are those with direct manufacturer relationships, better pricing, and tighter quality control.

For Sydney-based AV equipment rental companies, automotive part distributors, and precision engineering firms, direct factory access through WAG has delivered measurable results: reduced landed costs, faster sample turnaround, and fewer quality disputes after shipment.

## The WAG Factory Visit Model for Sydney Businesses

WAG structures factory visits in three phases:

**Phase 1: Verification and Quotation** — Before you travel, WAG confirms factory eligibility through background checks, financial stability reviews, and sample evaluation. You receive a detailed quotation with confirmed specifications.

**Phase 2: On-Ground Factory Visit** — WAG coordinates your itinerary in China, including factory meetings, production line inspections, and quality testing with your specific product requirements. Interpreters and technical staff accompany each visit.

**Phase 3: Post-Visit Coordination** — After the visit, WAG manages communication with the manufacturer, quality inspection during production, and logistics through to your Sydney warehouse.

For Sydney businesses, WAG's service covers the full cycle: you are not left managing a foreign manufacturer on your own after the visit.
