## What Perth Businesses Should Verify Before Visiting

**First, define your specifications precisely.** Chinese manufacturers produce to exact specifications—if your requirements are vague, the factory fills in the gaps with whatever is cheapest to produce. Bring technical drawings, tolerance specifications, and performance requirements to the visit.

**Second, research comparable pricing before you go.** You should have a realistic landed cost target before you sit down with a manufacturer. Without this benchmark, you have no reference point for evaluating quotes.

**Third, confirm the factory's export experience with Australian buyers.** Manufacturers that have supplied the Australian market before understand Australian packaging requirements, documentation standards, and quality expectations.
