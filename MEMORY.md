---
name: wag-article-image-rule
description: Every paragraph in WAG SEO articles needs a context-relevant illustration image
type: project
---

Every paragraph/section in a WAG SEO article requires a context-relevant illustration image, not just one cover image. Use `<FloatImage>` MDX component with contextual Unsplash images, floated to match paragraph content. Always use `marginInlineEnd` and `marginBlockStart/End` to add spacing between image and text — never let image touch text directly.
